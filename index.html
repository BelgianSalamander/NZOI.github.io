NZOI is the New Zealand Informations Olympiad, one of a number of Secondary School Olympiads operating under the umbrella organisation known as, NZ Science Olympiads.

The NZOI's goals are to:

  • Provide training oppurtunities in Informatics to selected New Zealand students who are currently enrolled in a secondary school in New Zealand, or have been enrolled in the last 8 months.
  • To select students for the International Olmpiad in Informatics (IOI).
  • - To foster informatics and programming in high schools accross New Zealand

The IOI is the foremost high school programming contest in the world. Each year, about August, it is hosted by a different country. New Zealand first sent a team in 2006, to the 18th Olympiad.
